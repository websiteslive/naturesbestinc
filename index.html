Who We Are

Owner-Operated, Missoula Born and Bred

Craig Best founded Nature's Best in 1995 after moving to Missoula following 8.5 years in the US Air Force. Starting with a single truck and trailer, he built the company into a team of 38 employees operating 24 trucks across the Greater Missoula Valley.

We specialize in providing complete, all-season lawn care for both commercial and residential customers — and we give every property our absolute best.

Our Promise

Professional Service You Can Count On

We believe the place where we live is an important factor in how we conduct our business. Our mission: timely, consistent, and professional service.

We Return Your Calls

Returning phone calls in a timely manner is rare in our industry — and we're proud of it. Supervisors carry cell phones in every truck, and Lori is available after hours until 9:00 PM at 406-544-4754.

High Employee Retention

We invest in our workforce with safety training, proper equipment, and competitive pay. Our high retention rate means you get familiar, consistent crews that know your property and care about quality.

Eco-Friendly Practices

We use Walker mowers for exceptional cut quality, compost all grass clippings and pruning debris, and offer organic-based fertilizer programs with EPA-registered products — no known carcinogens or groundwater risks.

Community First

Nature's Best donates to several local non-profit organizations and has been a proud Missoula Chamber member since 1995. Owner Craig Best served as an affiliate board member for NARPM and remains actively involved.
